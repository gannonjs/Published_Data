A simple description of the columns from left to right
Jonah Gannon
June 2023

The columns in UDG_data.csv from left to right are:

1) UDG primary designation used to identify it in individual_galaxy_notes
2) Environment: 1= cluster, 2=group, 3=field/filament
3) Distance [Mpc] - frequently assumed
4) Absolue V-band Magnitude [mag]
5) Average V-band surface brightness < \mu_V >_e within the half-light radius [mag arcsec^-2]
6) Stellar mass [ x 10^8 Solar Units]
7) 2-D Circularised Half-light Radius [kpc]
8) Recessional Velocity [km/s]
9) Uncertainty on Recessonal Velocity up [km/s]
10) Uncertainty on Recessional Velocity down [km/s]
11) Stellar velocity Dispersion [km/s]
12) Uncertainty on stellar velocity dispersion up [km/s]
13) Uncertainty on stellar velocity dispersion down [km/s]
14) Globular Cluster velocity dispersion [km/s] 
15) Uncertainty on globular cluster velocity dispersion up [km/s]
16) Uncertainty on globular cluster velocity dispersion down [km/s]
17) Number of globular clusters
18) Uncertainty on number of globular clusters up
19) Uncertainty on number of globular clusters down

Units are given in [square brackets]
Unknown values have been replaced with -1
