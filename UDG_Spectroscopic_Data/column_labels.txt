A simple description of the columns from left to right
Jonah Gannon
June 2023

The columns in UDG_data.csv from left to right are:

1) UDG primary designation used to identify it in individual_galaxy_notes
2) Secondary UDG designations
3) Environment: 1= cluster, 2=group, 3=field/filament
4) Distance [Mpc] - frequently assumed
5) Absolue V-band Magnitude [mag]
6) Average V-band surface brightness < \mu_V >_e within the half-light radius [mag arcsec^-2]
7) Stellar mass [ x 10^8 Solar Units]
8) 2-D Semi-major Half-light Radius [kpc]
9) Axial ratio b/a
10) Recessional Velocity [km/s]
11) Uncertainty on Recessonal Velocity + [km/s]
12) Uncertainty on Recessional Velocity - [km/s]
13) Stellar velocity Dispersion [km/s]
14) Uncertainty on stellar velocity dispersion + [km/s]
15) Uncertainty on stellar velocity dispersion - [km/s]
16) Globular Cluster velocity dispersion [km/s] 
17) Uncertainty on globular cluster velocity dispersion + [km/s]
18) Uncertainty on globular cluster velocity dispersion - [km/s]
19) Number of globular clusters
20) Uncertainty on number of globular clusters +
21) Uncertainty on number of globular clusters -
22) Mass weighted age [Gyr]
23) Uncertainty on mass weighted age + [Gyr]
24) Uncertainty on mass weighted age - [Gyr]
25) Mass weighted metallicity [Z/H] [dex]
26) Uncertainty on mass weighted metallicity [Z/H] + [dex]
27) Uncertainty on mass weighted metallicity [Z/H] - [dex]
28) Alpha enhancement [Mg/Fe] [dex]
29) Uncertainty on Alpha enhancement [Mg/Fe] + [dex]
30) Uncertainty on Alpha enhancement [Mg/Fe] - [dex]

Units are given in [square brackets] at the end
Unknown values have been replaced with -999
